frontend-nanodegree-arcade-game
===============================

**** CAT GAME!!!

- Use the up, down, left, right arrows to move your character
- Avoid the boots
- Get to the rooftops
- Enjoy!


(I suggest loosing all your 7 cat lives once just to see what happens!)
